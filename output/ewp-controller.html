ewp-controller

Diagram

Pierburg CWA400 (PWM version) controlled by MaxxECU RACE via PWM GPO. Phase 3 (07K engine). OEM pump 07K121011B impeller removed, housing stays as coolant manifold. No separate pump controller -- MaxxECU is the sole PWM driver. Cross-reference ewp-wiring-reference.md for full specs, part numbers, duty cycle map, and post-shutdown relay config. Confirmed: @wingman703 (07K/8HP build, identical drivetrain).
