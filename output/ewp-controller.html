ewp-controller

Diagram

Davies Craig Digital Controller (#8002 LCD) + EWP150 (40 LPM) harness. Phase 3 (07K engine). Replaces OEM belt-driven 07K water pump entirely. OEM pump 07K121011B excluded. Cross-reference schematics/ewp-controller.py for circuit schematic (current flow and switching logic). Installation notes: docs/ewp-wiring-reference.md
